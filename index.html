About

I am a ML and NLP research scientist at UKP Lab in TU Damrstadt working on Question Answering and Graph Neural Networks. Previously, I worked at the Coleridge Initiative where I help organize the Kaggle Competition Show US the Data. I got my master's degree from the School of Computing at KAIST, where I was research assistant at IR&NLP Lab and advised by Professor Sung-Hyon Myaeng.
